Valentine’s Day Nail Ideas

Express Your Love With These Stunning Designs

1. Classic Red & Pink Combo

You can never go wrong with the timeless duo of red and pink. Try alternating colors on each nail or go for an ombre effect for a soft, romantic vibe.

2. Hearts Galore

Tiny hearts, big hearts, glitter hearts—take your pick! Add heart accents to a neutral base or go all out with a heart-covered design. Perfect for the hopeless romantic!

3. Cute & Flirty French Tips

Give the classic French manicure a Valentine’s twist by swapping the white tips for pink, red, or even glitter. Add a tiny heart on the accent nail for an extra touch of love.

4. Love Letter Nails

Channel your inner romantic with nails that look like love letters! Think cursive writing, tiny envelopes, or even lipstick kiss marks. So sweet and sentimental!
